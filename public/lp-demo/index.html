Loss Prevention Dashboard - Mockup Demo

Four visual treatments of a new LP scorecard for Aubuchon Company. Sample data is pulled live from YODA (all 136 active stores, week of Apr 5–11, 2026). LP percentages are synthesized plausible values until real measures are wired up.

What you're looking at: Five KPI columns (Shrink, Overrides & Discounts, Coupons, Employee Discount, Everything Else) comparing every store to its tier benchmark. Three operational features are surfaced as UI elements: reason-code drilldown for shrink and O&D, plus a POS self-checkout watchlist. Store names, numbers, tiers, managers, and weekly sales dollars are real. LP percentages are synthesized for the mockup.
